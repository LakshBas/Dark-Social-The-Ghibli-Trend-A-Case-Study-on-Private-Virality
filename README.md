#Overview:

Analyzed the impact of “Dark Social” on digital content virality using the Ghibli trend as a case study. The project focused on understanding how aesthetic content spreads through private channels like WhatsApp and Instagram DMs and its influence on user engagement and marketing performance.

#Key Responsibilities:

-> Designed survey questionnaires to study private sharing behavior.
-> Collected and analyzed user engagement data.
-> Identified emotional and aesthetic factors driving content virality.
-> Built visual reports to interpret sharing patterns and user preferences.
-> Connected digital marketing concepts with real-world user behavior.


#Skills & Tools:

Excel, Power BI, Digital Marketing Analytics, Survey Analysis, Data Visualization

#Key Highlights from Analysis:

-> Majority of users shared Ghibli content through private messaging platforms.
-> Emotional and aesthetic appeal significantly increased sharing probability.
-> Dark social channels contributed to high engagement but low trackability.
-> Young users (18–30) were the most active in private sharing behavior.
-> Brands can leverage aesthetic storytelling to improve organic reach.
